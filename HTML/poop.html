Columbian mammoth's poop consists of 95% grasses and sedges, and varies from 0 to 25% woody plants between dung boluses, including saltbush, sagebrush, water birch, and blue spruce. It was very big, if you compare mammoth's poop with the size of poop of an elephant it's 8 times as big.



Paleontologists have found Columbian mammoth poop in several large caves in the southwest. The poop contains pieces of grass and other plants.

One particular cave, Bechan cave in southern Utah, has a dung blanket 16 inches thick with a total volume of 8,000 cubic feet ! This may seem like a lot, but if you think about it, an African elephant drops 200-300 pounds of poop a day! So, a group of mammoths could have easily covered the bottom of Bechan cave in just a few year
